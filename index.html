Life-changing decision

In October 2023, after nearly a decade in corporate analytics, I made a deliberate choice to step away — not to escape, but to explore. What followed was two years of independent travel across more than 20 countries, through Europe, Asia, the Americas, and Oceania.

Travel taught me things no office could. Navigating unfamiliar systems, communicating across language barriers, adapting plans on the fly — these became second nature. I learned to read people quickly, to find common ground with strangers, and to stay composed when nothing goes to plan. That cross-cultural fluency now shapes how I connect with clients, students, and colleagues from every background.

The road also clarified what mattered most to me: movement, health, and being genuinely useful to people. By the time I arrived in Sydney, I knew exactly what I wanted to do next — and I had the qualifications to back it up.
